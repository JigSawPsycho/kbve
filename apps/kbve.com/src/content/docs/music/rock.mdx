## Rock

Rock is a broad genre of popular music that originated in the United States in the late 1940s and early 1950s.
It evolved from earlier genres such as rock and roll and rhythm and blues and typically features a strong backbeat, the use of electric guitars, bass, and drums, and often, lyrics centered around themes of rebellion, love, and social issues. Rock music encompasses a wide range of styles and subgenres, including classic rock, hard rock, punk rock, and alternative rock, each with its distinct characteristics and cultural significance.
Pominent artists in rock history include The Beatles, Led Zeppelin, The Rolling Stones, and Nirvana.
